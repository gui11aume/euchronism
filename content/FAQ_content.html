Euchronism FAQ

What is euchronism?

Euchronism is the art of doing things at the right moment.

Why should I use euchronism?

Euchronism helps you keep track of your thoughts over time. Contrary to a task scheduler, which helps you think about something, euchronism helps you forget about it (and relax).

What is a lightray?

A lightray is a message that you can address to future you. You can decide the time scale but not thre precise time when future you will receive it.
