Euchronism FAQ

What is euchronism?

Euchronism is the art of doing things at the right moment.

Why should I use euchronism?

Euchronism helps you keep track of your thoughts over time. Contrary to task schedulers, which help you think about something, euchronism helps you forget about it and relax.

What is a chronicle?

A chronicle is personal message that you can address to yourself in the future.

What can I send myself in a chronicle

You can only send a text message.
